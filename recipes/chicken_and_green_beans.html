Recipe
Ingredients

  • chicken 2
  • green beans 1 bag
  • onion 1
  • garlic 4 tsp
  • ginger 4 tsp or 4 squirts

    • Sauce

  • soy sauce 4 tbsp
  • dry sherry 2 tbsp
  • sesame oil 2 tsp
  • corn starch 4 tsp
  • red pepper flakes 1/4 tsp
  • sugar 2 tsp
Steps

  • 1. put rice to cook
  • 2. wash and trim green beans then put green beans to boil for 15 min
  • 3. cut chicken into small cubes
  • 4. mix sauce and add to chicken and mix
  • 5. cut onion into large squares
  • 6. add oil to pan and throw in chicken when it heats up
  • 7. add oil in another pan and throw in the onions when it heats up
  • 8. when onions are half cooked add garlic 4 tsp and ginger 4 squirts to onions and mix
  • 9. check green beans and drain them and add to chicken
  • 10. when onions are see through add them to the chicken
  • 11. mix everything together
