Breaded Chicken and Corn Rice
Ingredients

  • chicken 2
  • egg 1 bag
  • breaad crumbs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • rice
  • corn 1 cup
  • onion
  • garlic
  • butter
  • chicken bouillon powder

Steps

  • 1. put rice to cook (2 cups)
  • 1. mix up one egg in a bowl
  • 1. spread out bread crumbs on a flat plate
  • 1. add oil to pan and set to low-med heat
  • 3. cut each chicken piece into two thin pieces and dry with a papertowel
  • 4. season each side with salt, pepper, then dunk it in the egg, then in the bread crumbs
  • 5. add 3 tbsp of butter into a small pot on low-med heat and let it melt
  • 1. cut onion into small pieces
  • 6. add onion to pot with melted butter
  • 7. check on the chicken pieces and flip if needed
  • 1. add 1 tbsp of garlic in with the onions
  • 1. set pieces of chicken aside on a plate and paper towel when done
  • 1. when rice is cooked add it to the pot with the onions and garlic and mix
  • 1. add in 1 cup of corn to the rice and mix
  • 1. turn off the heat and add 1 tbsp of the green chicken bouillon powder and mix
