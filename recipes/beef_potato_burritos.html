Beef and Potato Burrito
Ingredients

  • ground beef 1 lb
  • taco seasoning packets 2
  • potatoes 3-4
  • salt
  • pepper
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • cayenne pepper (spicy)
  • paprika

Steps

  • 1. rinse and cut 3 potatoes into small cubes
  • 2. add the peices into a bowl and add 2 tbsp of oil and season with salt, pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, and paprika and mix everything together
  • 3. put in air fryer: 360 degrees for 15 min (check at 12 min)
  • 4. heat up a new pan and throw in the ground beef (no oil needed)
  • 5. add in 1/4 cup of water and two taco seasoning packets
  • 6. mix it all together until its cooked on low-med heat
  • 7. cook tortilla and add cheese and sour cream
