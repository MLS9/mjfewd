Salmon
Ingredients

  • salmon 6
  • broccoli or brussel sprouts 1 bag
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder

Steps

  • 1. put rice to cook
  • 2. preheat oven to 415 degrees
  • 3. dry each piece with a papertowel and check for bones
  • 4. season each side with salt, pepper, and garlic powder
    or
    salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder
  • 5. put a baking sheet and lay down each piece with the skin side down
  • 6. put in oven for 20 min
  • 7. cook broccoli 10 minutes later (so it doesnt get cold)
  • 8. cook brussel sprouts in the microwave for 3 min (half of whatever its says in the bag)
    melt half a stick of butter in a bowl and cover in plastic (10-15 seconds only)
    put brussel sprouts in a large bowl and add the melted butter, salt, pepper, and garlic powder
    line the air fryer with foil (optional - don't cover the sides completely)
    cook at 360 degrees for 8 min and if they need more time do 2 more minutes
