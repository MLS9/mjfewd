Recipe
Ingredients
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup cold butter
  • 2/3 cup whole milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Fresh strawberries
  • Whipped cream
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in cold butter until crumbly.
  4. Stir in milk and vanilla to form dough.
  5. Drop dough onto baking sheet. Bake 12–15 mins.
  6. Let cool. Slice and layer with strawberries and whipped cream.
